## English

### What is this?

A space for sharing research, ideas, and experiments at the intersection of **AI, Web3, and design systems**. Built with AI collaboration in mind.

### Who is it for?

- **Builders starting from zero** — If you're learning to code, exploring Web3, or wondering how AI can extend what you can do, the notes here might help.
- **People who prefer structure over noise** — Research, frameworks, and clear thinking over hype.
- **Long-term thinkers** — For those who care more about systems and capability than short-term trends.

### What you'll find

- Research on Web3 infrastructure and protocol design
- Reflections on AI-augmented workflows and tooling
- Practical learnings from building with Cursor and modern tools

### Why it exists

To document a path from designer to builder, and to share what works when you combine structure, systems, and AI.

### 适合谁？

- **从 0 开始的构建者** — 如果你在学编程、探索 Web3，或想了解 AI 如何放大你的能力，这里的博客或许有用。

### 你会看到什么

- Web3 基础设施与协议设计的研究

记录从设计师到构建者的路径，分享在结构、系统与 AI 结合时，什么真正有效。
